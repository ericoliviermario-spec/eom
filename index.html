Some people map a journey. I'm still on mine.

Music found me during one of the quietest periods of my life — not as a career move, but as a way through. What began as an escape became a language. What became a language became a body of work.

Eric Olivier Mario is electronic music rooted in feeling — melodic, atmospheric, built for the kind of listening that turns inward. My compositions don't follow a genre so much as a direction: from the weight of what is unresolved, toward the openness of what becomes possible.

That direction has a shape. Three movements. A journey from C minor to D major — from darkness into light.

And because I'm still walking it, I guide other artists through the same arc. Not from above. From beside.

If you make music and feel the pull toward something deeper than technique — toward who you actually are when you create — this space was made for you.
