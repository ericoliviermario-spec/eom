Module I

Rooted Creation

Discover your creative foundation — a single session to begin.

Where you begin

You feel the pull to create but the tools feel unfamiliar, or the sounds you reach for don't feel like yours. Maybe you are starting from scratch entirely.

There is a gap between who you are and what you make — and you are ready to close it.

Where this leads

In a single 2-hour session, you reconnect with your authentic creative voice — exploring your identity, your influences, and what exists underneath them.

You leave with a first project built from scratch in Logic Pro, a clearer sense of direction, and a written summary of your next steps.

What happens inside the session

Part 1

Unearthing

We begin by discovering your authentic voice — exploring your creativity, your past influences, and what exists underneath them. Finding the artistic identity and references that are truly yours.

Part 2

Creation

You learn to create a project from scratch in Logic Pro. I teach you the techniques to start putting your ideas together — session setup, signal flow, and the essentials of building sound from nothing.

Part 3

First Movements

Translating your insights into sound. Experimenting with initial compositions that reflect your unique emotional landscape — slowly, without judgment, without reference.

What you walk away with
  • A reference track that reflects your emerging sound identity
  • A first Logic Pro project started from scratch — your very first sonic sketch
  • A recorded session summary capturing your key insights, references, and direction
  • Clarity on your 3 core influences and how to translate them into your own sound
  • A creative brief — a one-page document defining your artist identity, mood, and sonic direction
  • A next steps roadmap — what to work on in the weeks ahead
  • Confidence that you have something real to build from
After this module you will
  • Complete one finished track
  • Understand your harmonic language
  • Develop a personal sonic identity
