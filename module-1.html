Module I

Rooted Creation

Reconnecting to your authentic self — and building from the ground up.

Where you begin

You feel the pull to create but the tools feel unfamiliar, or the sounds you reach for don't feel like yours. Maybe you have been creating from influence and expectation — or maybe you are starting from scratch entirely.

Either way, there is a gap between who you are and what you make.

Where this leads

A solid creative foundation — understanding your tools, your DAW, your signal flow — paired with a reconnection to the rhythmic, emotional, and cultural roots that are authentically yours.

You learn to build sound from nothing and begin creating from what you actually carry.

What happens inside

Week 1

Unearthing

We begin by discovering your authentic voice — exploring your creativity, your past influences, and what exists underneath them. You also get grounded in the essentials: session setup, signal flow, and how your DAW works.

Week 2

Building Sound

An introduction to sound creation — learning techniques to generate unique audio elements from nothing. You explore synthesis, sampling, and layering to begin crafting sonic textures that are distinctly yours.

Week 3

First Movements

You learn to create a project from scratch. Putting your ideas together using the techniques you have built — translating your inner landscape into initial compositions, slowly, without judgment.

Week 4

Integration

Bringing it all together into a cohesive piece. Arrangement, shaping atmosphere, space, and emotion. The cohort witnesses each other's integration work.
