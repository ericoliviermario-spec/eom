Module I

Rooted Creation

Finding what is yours before you build anything else.

Where you begin

You have been creating — perhaps for years — but something feels borrowed. The sounds you reach for, the structures you default to, the identity you present as an artist. None of it feels fully yours.

There is a gap between who you are and what you make.

Where this leads

A reconnection to the rhythmic, emotional, and cultural foundations that are authentically yours. Not a new style — a deeper root.

You begin creating from what you actually carry, not what you have absorbed from the world around you.

What happens inside

Week 1

Unearthing

We begin by identifying what is borrowed. Listening to your own work with new ears. Mapping the influences that have shaped you — and finding what exists underneath them.

Week 2

Sitting With

A week of restraint. Creating less. Listening more. Allowing the silence between your habits to reveal what has been waiting underneath.

Week 3

First Movements

From the stillness, something begins to emerge. This week you create from the roots you have uncovered — slowly, without judgment, without reference.

Week 4

Integration

Bringing what you have found into your existing practice. Not replacing your tools — but grounding them in something real. The cohort witnesses each other's integration pieces.
