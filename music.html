The Arc

Six passages.

One movement from dark to light.

Begin below ↓
I

I. The Descent

Self-Discovery

Beneath the Surface  — 4:32
4:32
"The first step is always down. Before you find anything, you lose the story you were told about yourself."

What did you believe about yourself before you started questioning?

II

II. The Awakening

Awakening

First Light 5:18
5:18
"Something stirs. Not understanding yet — just the sense that what was solid has begun to move."

When did you first feel that something in you was changing?

III. The Encounter

Presence

Face to Face 6:04
6:04
"Presence is not peace. It is the willingness to feel what is actually here."

What are you avoiding feeling right now?

IV

IV. The Embodiment

Embodiment

Rooted  — 5:47
5:47
"The body already knows. The work is learning to stop overriding it."

Where in your body do you feel your creative impulse begin?

V

V. The Devotion

Devotion

Without Reason 4:56
4:56
"Devotion is not loyalty to an outcome. It is the willingness to keep showing up without one."

What would you continue creating even if no one ever heard it?

VI

VI. The Union

Union

Arriving 7:12
7:12
"Not the end. The beginning of creating from a place you no longer need to explain."

What does your music sound like when you have nothing to prove?

The arc does not end with listening.

If you are an artist moving through your own version of this passage — there is a space built for that work.
