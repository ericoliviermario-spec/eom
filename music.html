Presence

Three movements.

One arc from dark into arrival.

I

Movement I

The Descent Beneath

Inward Motion

The music you make when you're still underground — feeling your way through the dark without knowing what's ahead.

"The first step is always down. Before you find anything, you lose the story you were told about yourself."
II

Movement II

The Threshold Continuum

Music to Stay With

The suspended place between who you were and who you're becoming. Neither darkness nor light — pure transition.

"Something stirs. Not understanding yet — just the sense that what was solid has begun to move."
III

Movement III

The Return Release

When the Weight Lifts

The music that arrives when you stop fighting. Not happiness — something quieter and more permanent than that.

"Not the end. The beginning of creating from a place you no longer need to explain."

The arc does not end with listening.

If you are an artist moving through your own version of this passage — there is a space built for that work.
