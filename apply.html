Application

Begin here.

Before we begin working together, a few questions — not to qualify you, but to understand where you are.

There are no right answers. Only honest ones.

What is your name and artist name?

If they are the same, that is fine.

Describe your creative practice in a few sentences.

What do you make? How long have you been making it?

Where are you right now in your artistic life?

Not your career — your inner relationship to creating.

What brought you to this page?

Be as specific or as abstract as you like.

What would need to shift in your life to make space for this kind of work over the next 4–6 weeks?

Which module resonates most right now?

How did you find this space?

How should we reach you?

How would you prefer to have a conversation?

Is there anything else you want to share before we begin?

This is optional.

Thank you.

You will hear back within 7 days.

If there is alignment, we will schedule a conversation. If not, you will receive an honest and respectful reply.

No automated responses. Every application is read personally.
