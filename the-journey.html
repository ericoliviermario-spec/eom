The Journey

Training for artists
who are ready to stop
performing and start arriving.

This is not education. It is excavation.

Most artist development focuses on skill, audience, and output.

This work focuses on something underneath all of that: who you are when you create.

The Journey is a guided passage through two territories — from finding your creative foundation and reconnecting to your authentic voice, to inhabiting presence through sound and building something entirely yours.

It is not for everyone. It is for artists who already sense that their next evolution is not technical.

What shifts when you do this work

  • A clearer sense of who you are when you create — and why that matters more than any technique
  • The ability to finish what you start, because you're no longer creating against yourself
  • A sound that is recognisably yours — not assembled from references, but built from what you actually carry
  • The confidence to sit with uncertainty in the creative process without abandoning the work
  • A relationship with music that feels sustainable — not driven by output, but by genuine presence

Module I

Rooted Creation

Discover your creative foundation — a single session to reconnect with your authentic voice.

A 2-hour discovery session for new students. Together we unearth your influences, build a project from scratch in Logic Pro or the DAW of your choice, and define your sonic direction.

You walk away with a reference track, a creative brief defining your artist identity, and a next steps roadmap.

From starting with nothing  →  to building from what you actually carry.

Single session  ·  2 hours  ·  1-to-1  ·  £69

Module II

Presence & Sound

Building depth through weekly practice — crafting a complete track that is entirely yours.

Four weekly 1-to-1 sessions that take you from grounding and listening to designing your own sounds and completing a full composition. Each week builds on the last.

You walk away with a completed reference track, a personal sound palette, a listening log, and direct access to Eric throughout via phone and WhatsApp.

From scattered ideas  →  to a cohesive creation rooted in who you are.

4 weekly sessions  ·  1-to-1  ·  Async support  ·  £199/month

The Space

This space is for artists who —

  • Are seeking personal transformation through their creative work
  • Want to learn music production from the ground up while developing their unique voice
  • Are ready to expand their creativity and build a cohesive sonic universe
  • Are willing to slow down to go deeper
  • Are drawn to this page for reasons they may not fully articulate yet

This is not the right fit if you —

  • Are only looking for production techniques without the inner work
  • Want to grow your audience or streaming numbers
  • Need external validation to continue creating
  • Are not willing to invest time in weekly practice
  • Want a curriculum without the creative self-discovery

Enrollment is by application only.

This is not about gatekeeping. It is about ensuring the space holds what it needs to hold.
