Module III

From Artist
to Universe

When the work becomes larger than you.

Where you begin

You have done the inner work. You create from presence, from your roots, from honesty. But something in you senses there is another threshold.

A place where the work stops being about self-expression and becomes something offered. You are ready for creation as devotion.

Where this leads

The experience of creating as an act of service. Not performing for an audience, not expressing for yourself — but offering something through you that is larger than you.

Your art becomes a transmission.

What happens inside

Week 1

Surrender

Releasing attachment to outcome, identity, and recognition. What remains when you stop needing your art to do something for you?

Week 2

Emptying

Creating from nothing. No references, no templates, no familiar patterns. Starting each session from true zero.

Week 3

Receiving

Shifting from maker to channel. Practices that open you to what wants to come through, rather than what you want to produce.

Week 4

Devotion

Establishing creation as daily practice — not productive routine, but devotional act. What does it mean to sit down each day and offer?

Week 5

Integration Piece

Beginning the final work that will be shared with the cohort. Not a showcase — a witnessed offering. Working with whatever has emerged through the process.

Week 6

Witnessing

Sharing and being witnessed. The cohort gathers to receive each other's integration pieces. Closing the container with care and reverence.
